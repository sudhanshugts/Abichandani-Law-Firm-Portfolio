Anahita Brahmbhatt

Director

Anahita is an architect, developer and director at Carbon Cycles. She simultaneously holds the role of Joint Director of New Business at Bsafal, a real estate development firm based in Ahmedabad. Previously, she has worked for David Chipperfield Architects in London. She graduated with a Masters Degree from the Architectural Association School of Architecture in 2022. Her work operates in the realm of land, economy, strategic planning as well as climate innovation. She also wishes to create housing for 1.77 million homeless people in India.

Sahir Patel

Director

Sahir is an Architect and a researcher whose work operates through remote sensing and at the intersection of research, advocacy, ecological planning and contemporary spatial practices. His thesis project at the Architectural Association - ‘Turbulent Displacements’ - is an ongoing body of work that addresses alternative imaginaries in relation to large scale water and land management infrastructure in Gujarat.

Sahir has previously worked with multidisciplinary research studio - Territorial Agency, based in London.Sahir has been working on a variety of land design projects in Gujarat at various scales - from master planning, permaculture, rewilding, water management, landscaping and real estate projects around Ahmedabad. They use integrated and whole systems thinking to respond to existing climate, topography, water, ecology and social conditions to optimise their relations to design ecosystems that are bio-diverse, resilient, and productive.

Aakash Shah

Director

Aakash Shah is an engineer, developer and director at Carbon Cycles. He simultaneously works at Neptune Textile Mills, a medical textiles company in Ahmedabad. Having previously worked in Germany, he aims to regrow the arid regions of India into something close to the black forest using biochar.
